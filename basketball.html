INTRODUCTION

What is Basketball?

Basketball is a team sport where two teams of five players try to score points by shooting a ball through the opponent's hoop.

The game was invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith. Today, it is one of the most popular sports worldwide, played in professional leagues, schools, and local courts.
